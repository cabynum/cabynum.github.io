I'm an engineering manager at Red Hat, leading the Data Processing team within RHOAI (Red Hat OpenShift AI). My team builds the data infrastructure that powers AI/ML workflows at scale.

I've spent my career at the intersection of infrastructure, consulting, and product engineering. Ten years at Red Hat Consulting, a stint building with startups at Levvel, and back to Red Hat building the platform layer.

Right now I'm focused on two things: leading my team and building personal AI systems that actually work. Argus is my engineering management command center - an experiment in how far you can push AI-assisted productivity when you take it seriously as a systems problem, not just a chat interface.

I write about what I learn along the way.
