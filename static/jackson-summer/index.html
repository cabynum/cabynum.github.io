About the Need to Read Guide

This is a short worksheet you fill in while you're reading The Outsiders — not after. A few questions each day. It trains your brain to pay attention and remember what you read, which makes the AR test easy.

You hand it in before taking your AR test.

AR Testing

AR stands for Accelerated Reader. It's a short multiple-choice quiz you take on a computer at school after you finish a book. Takes about 15 minutes. It just checks that you read and understood the book — not a big deal.

You have to go to campus to take these.

The library is open on specific days this summer. Pick a day after you finish a book and show up — no appointment needed. Bring your GAC laptop if you have one.
