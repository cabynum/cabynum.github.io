What is AR Testing?

AR (Accelerated Reader) is a short quiz you take on campus after finishing a book. It's multiple choice, about 15-20 minutes, and just checks that you read and understood it. Not a big deal — just show up to the library on one of the open days.

Need to Read Guide

Required for The Outsiders — use the "Need to Read Fiction" version. Fill it in daily while reading, not after you finish. You must hand it in before your AR test.

Why? It trains your brain to ask questions and remember what you read. Makes the AR test way easier.
